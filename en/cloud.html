Cloud is the new normal

Cloud computing is the main driver for the Digital Transformation of any enterprise
The Cloud makes IT an opportunity for growth rather than a hindrance

Cloud defined

Cloud comuting is the latest paradigm in IT: big cloud providers are building data centers all around the world, achieving global economies of scale. These data centers are used to provision IT infrastructure or services to clients. The Cloud philosophy is based on an on-demand access model: thanks to the Cloud it is possible to reduce hardware costs and time to market for IT projects, scale infrastructure rapidly using cutting-edge technology, all while maintaining complete governance over your IT resources.

Soluzioni Futura is an Amazon Web Services Consulting Partner. AWS is the leader among Cloud providers and it sets the standard for other technology vendors

Our solutions

Working with a public Cloud provider who's part of a partner ecosystem, our clients enjoy a level of governance over their IT resources which is only achievable by having all of their infrastructure on-premises, but with the opportunity of working with otherwise difficult to obtain, hard to scale or very costly technology, with little-to-none investment in hardware or additional personnel.

We strive to offer our clients the best solutions. We work collaboratively with our clients' IT departments or their contractors, giving support, consultancy and training.
